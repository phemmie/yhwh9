Company History

YHWH and Partners W. A. Limited is an Engineering and Construction Company set up about 16years ago to provide services in the under listed areas:

• Civil Infrastructure e.g. building, environment, Concrete & steel structures, transportation & highway.

• Independent power project.

• Project management.

We bring together men, materials and machinery for an integrated approach to engineering infrastructure development, management & construction and we are capable of tapping from a wide range of professional skills in the engineering field, hence, we have the flexibility to act depending on the circumstances of a given project as consultants, turnkey contractor main/sub contractor and co-developer, or project engineers/managers.
